Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

YourDealsUK ("we", "us") operates the website yourdealsuk.co.uk and an associated Telegram channel. This policy explains, in plain terms, what data we handle. We keep it minimal by design.

What we collect

Our website is a static page. We do not run accounts, logins, or forms, and we do not ask you for personal information. We do not directly collect names, email addresses, or payment details through the site.

  • Hosting logs: our host may record standard technical data (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability. We do not use this to identify you.

  • Telegram: if you join our channel, your use of Telegram is governed by Telegram's own privacy policy, not ours.

Affiliate links

We participate in affiliate programmes, including the Amazon Associates Programme and Awin. When you click a deal link and make a purchase, the retailer or network may set cookies to attribute the sale, and we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. We do not receive your payment or personal details from these purchases — only aggregate commission reporting.

Cookies

Our own site does not set tracking cookies. Affiliate networks and retailers you click through to may set their own cookies under their respective policies.

Your choices

You can leave the Telegram channel at any time. You can block cookies in your browser settings and use tracking-protection tools if you wish.

Children

Changes
